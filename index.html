About Us

Cooking should be easy for everyone—including non-verbal individuals with autism. That’s why we created CookEasy, a mobile app designed to help users prepare meals independently with a simple, structured, and accessible approach.

  • Step-by-step visual guides for easy meal preparation
  • Personalized recipes based on user preferences
  • A clean, distraction-free interface that’s easy to navigate

We believe that with the right support, anyone can gain confidence in the kitchen. CookEasy is built to make cooking less overwhelming and more empowering for those who need it most.

Empowering Independence with CookEasy

CookEasy is designed to help non-verbal individuals with autism prepare meals with ease. Through visual communication and step-by-step guidance, the app fosters confidence in the kitchen.

01 What is CookEasy and how does it help?

CookEasy is a mobile app designed to assist non-verbal individuals with autism in preparing simple meals. It focuses on cognitive accessibility and independence in meal preparation through visual communication features.

02 How does CookEasy improve the cooking experience?

The app provides step-by-step visual guides, interactive instructions, and customizable meal plans to help users follow recipes independently.

03 How does CookEasy enhance cooking independence for non-verbal individuals?

CookEasy promotes autonomy by offering a structured, easy-to-follow approach to meal preparation, reducing reliance on caregivers while ensuring a safe and guided cooking process.
